# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 10, 2026

## Overview

AniKai Mobile respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Local Storage Only
All your data is stored **locally on your device**:
- Bookmarks
- Watch history
- App settings
- PIN (if enabled)
- Download tracking
- Video progress timestamps

### No Server Storage
We do NOT:
- Store your data on our servers
- Track your viewing habits
- Share your information with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking cookies

## Permissions

The app requests these permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Internet | Stream anime content |
| Storage | Save download tracking |
| Notifications | Optional episode alerts |
| Wakelock | Keep screen on while watching |

## Third-Party Content

The app displays content from AniKai.to. Please review their privacy policy for information about how they handle data.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, open an issue on GitHub.

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.
